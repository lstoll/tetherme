Welcome to TetherMe

This site will help you get tethering up and running on your phone. Either choose your carrier from the list, or click enter settings and type in the APN details. When you submit, you phone will prompt you to install the profile. Once installed, look in Settings -> General -> Network -> Tethering. Turn it on, and you are right to go! (Make sure you are running iTunes 8.2). If the option isn't there, try restarting your phone.
